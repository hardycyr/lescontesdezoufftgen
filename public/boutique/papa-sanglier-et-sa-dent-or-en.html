Papa Sanglier et sa dent en or

Volume 3 of the collection The Tales of Zoufftgen

Illustrated picture book · Hardcover · €19

Order the book

In the forest of Zoufftgen, Papa Sanglier never imagined that a simple walk would turn into such an adventure… From dentist to ophthalmologist, this grumpy dad comes back transformed… and very, very proud of his new golden look!

Inspired by a real place

This tale will let you discover the forester’s house of Zoufftgen, a place that has existed since the end of the 19th century.

In this album:

  • an illustrated story
  • a page inspired by a real place in Zoufftgen
  • activities to extend the reading
  • an information page about wild boars
  • hardcover, 3D gilding, square format 21 × 21 cm, 32 pages
  • read-aloud from age 3
  • independent reading from age 6-7
