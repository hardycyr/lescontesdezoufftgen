La maison du petit zouffarceur

Volume 2 of the collection The Tales of Zoufftgen

Villagers grow worried: the hazelnuts left on windowsills are no longer disappearing… A new mystery enters Zoufftgen, between everyday village life and a touch of wonder.

Returning to Zoufftgen

In this second volume, the world of the village deepens. A familiar habit fades away, and that absence is enough to make the villagers start looking at what surrounds them in a new way.

A story that extends the first tale and continues to weave a link between the real places of the village and the imagination they inspire.

A book conceived as an object

This book is crafted with care:

  • 32 pages, hardcover
  • 3D gilding and selective varnish on the cover
  • thick, matte-finish interior paper

A book to read, to give, and to pass on.
