The collection

Children's picture books to read, to keep, and to pass on.

The Tales of Zoufftgen is a collection of children's picture books written and illustrated by Hélène Hardy. Each book is rooted in a real place of the village, and extends the story through drawing, discovery and sharing.

Books designed as objects

Each book is crafted with care: square format 21 × 21 cm, hardcover, thick paper, delicate finishes. Books designed to be reread, offered and kept.

A tale-workshop to share

Beyond the illustrated story, each book invites the child to extend the tale: learning to draw a character, discovering a real place, observing nature, or sharing a recipe inspired by the story.

Draw

A step-by-step to make the characters your own.

Discover

A link to a real place or an element of nature.

Share

A recipe, a gesture or a moment to share together.

Book after book, The Tales of Zoufftgen weave a shared world where the real places of the village become the starting point of new stories.
