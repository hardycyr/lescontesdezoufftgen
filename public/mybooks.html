The collection

A collection rooted in real places

The Tales of Zoufftgen form a collection of children's books, written and illustrated by Hélène Hardy. Each story takes root in a real place of the village, between memory and imagination.

A carefully crafted edition

The books in the collection are designed with particular care:

  • hardcover
  • thick, matte-finish interior paper
  • delicate finishes (selective varnish, gilding)

Each book is conceived as an object in its own right, meant to be kept, given, and passed on.

Where to get the books

The books are available:

  • by online order (postal delivery)
  • by pickup in Zoufftgen, by appointment
  • at the artisans' shop Artisan'Arts in Volmerange-les-Mines

A growing collection

The world of The Tales of Zoufftgen continues to grow. New tales will join the collection, alongside objects inspired by the stories, made in small batches.
