News & events

The world of The Tales of Zoufftgen comes alive through readings, encounters and exchanges with children, families and cultural venues of the region.

Through these moments, the stories slowly leave the pages to join the places, the voices, and the memories.

Events & readings

Library of Dudelange — Luxembourg

Reading of the two tales of Le Petit Zouffarceur, followed by a time of exchange and a drawing workshop around the characters of the story.

School readings & after-school venues

Times of reading, exchange and discovery around the tales, nature, and local heritage.

Meet-and-greets & book signings

Moments of exchange with readers, during local meet-and-greets and book signing sessions.
