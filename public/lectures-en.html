Meetings and readings

I occasionally offer reading sessions and meetings based on the Tales of Zoufftgen, aimed at children and families.

These moments are designed to be calm and poetic interludes, conducive to listening, imagination, and discovering stories, illustrations, and the world around them. They are a continuation of my work as an author and illustrator, rooted in nature, the land, and the passing on of knowledge.

üåø Available formats

Time for reading and discussion

Reading of a story followed by a discussion with the children about the story, the characters, the forest, the animals, or the profession of author-illustrator.
Approximate duration: 60 minutes.

A gathering around imagination and nature

Depending on the context, reading can be followed by a discussion or simple creative activity (imagining what lives in a tree, an animal, a place, etc.), adapted to the children's age.

Formats are adjusted according to the audience, venue, and setting.
